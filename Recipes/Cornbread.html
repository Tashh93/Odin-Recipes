Cornbread

As you can see, I LOVE southern style foods. Cornbread is my go to when all else fails when it comes to bread for dinner.

Here is the recipe:

  • cornmeal
  • flour
  • sugar
  • milk
  • eggs
  • sour cream
  • butter
  • oil

Here are the steps of cooking:

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together cornmeal, flour and sugar
  3. add milk to beaten egg and then add egg mixture to the dry ingredients
  4. Add sour cream and whisk together and finally add melted butter and whisk until well combined.
  5. Heat large cast iron skillet over high heat.
  6. add oil to skillet and then remove from the pan.
  7. Pour mixture into skillet and bake for 35-45 minutes or golden brown.
  8. remove skillet from oven and let cool for 20 minutes
  9. allow to cool and then serve. ENJOY!
